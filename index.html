Workflow Replay

Reply → Database → AI → Agent Alert → Viewer

A simple operating loop showing how Laura turns an inbound lead reply into structured context and a recommended next action.

01

Reply

Mary replies: “Interested, looking to sell in the next 90 days.”

02

Database

Laura matches the reply to the correct lead record and stores the new signal.

03

AI

Laura identifies seller intent, timeline, priority, reason, and recommended action.

04

Agent Alert

The agent receives a concise alert with the reply, summary, priority, and next step.

05

Viewer

The operational viewer shows the updated status and what should happen next.

What Laura is proving

Clarity Before Automation

Who is this for?

Laura is for real estate agents and small teams who need to stay responsive across leads, clients, notes, and follow-up tasks.

What problem are we solving?

Laura helps prevent important client details, replies, and next steps from getting lost in the noise of daily real estate communication.

What outcome improves?

Laura helps agents follow up faster, prioritize better, and act with more confidence on the right opportunity at the right time.

Operating principle

Decision cockpit, not dashboard dump.

The Laura Operational Viewer is designed to surface attention, reason, risk, and next action — not just display raw activity.
